Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil
  • Frozen khoya
  • Cardmon, Saffron
  • Milk,water

Recipe

  1. Defrost frozen khoya and crumble it to powder and measure Â¼ cup .
  2. Trim brown sides of the bread and place remaning portion in a blender and grind.
  3. Mix khoya and the bread crumbs,knead it sprinkling water/milk
  4. Roll into equal sized balls.Heat oil/ ghee in a kadai and fry in medium flame until brown in colour.
  5. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, cardamon, saffron.
  6. Drop the fried bread balls in hot sugar syrup and let it get soaked for 1 or two hours.

Samosa

Ingredients

  • Maida flour
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Potatoes
  • Spice powders
  • Ginger,garlic,peas,onion

Recipe

  1. Knead the dough till it gets smooth. Cover with a plate and leave it rest for 30 mins.
  2. Add cumin seeds,crushed ginger,garlic,green chilli,onions,salt,peas,all spice powders in oil and saute well. Add mashed potato and mix.
  3. Divide the dough and filling into equal parts.Take one part of the dough and spread into a thin circle, Cut in half
  4. Take on semi cricle and make cone shape.
  5. Take a portion of potato filling and place it in the middle and close the upper part of cone shape.
